Sanskrit Anaphora Resolution System

The Sanskrit Anaphora Resolution System (SARS) was developed as part of M.Phil. research by Madhav Gopal (M.Phil. 2009-2011) under the supervision of Prof. Anvita Abbi and co-supervision of Dr. Girish Nath Jha. The program coding has been done by Dr. Girish Nath Jha and Kshitij Arora. The Devanagari input mechanism has been developed in JavaScript by Satyendra Kumar Chaube, Dharm Singh Rathore and Dr. Girish Nath Jha.
